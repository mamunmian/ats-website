Institution Overview

About Aynapur Technical School

Aynapur Technical School (ATS) was founded in 2015 under the guidance of the Abdul Aziz Master Memorial and Habibur Rahman Welfare Trust. It is situated in Aynapur village, Tangail Sadar, Tangail, Bangladesh, the institution has been delivering quality technical education for over a decade. It has consistently upheld high standards in academic excellence, discipline, and moral integrity.

Aynapur Technical School operates under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) and strictly adheres to the curriculum approved by the board. The school is dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals who demonstrate not only academic achievement but also strong character, leadership skills, and a commitment to serving the nation.

Mission Statement

To deliver quality education that nurtures the intellectual, physical, moral, and social development of students, empowering them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of Bangladesh.

Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Bangladesh, known for academic excellence, character development and producing graduates who contribute positively to the society.
